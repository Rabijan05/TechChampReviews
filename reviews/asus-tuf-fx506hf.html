Asus TUF Gaming F15 FX506HF Review

Overview

The Asus TUF Gaming F15 FX506HF is a mid-range gaming laptop built for solid everyday performance and gaming at 1080p. It pairs a capable Intel CPU with an NVIDIA RTX GPU and a fast 144 Hz display — making it a strong choice for gamers and creators on a budget.

Key Specifications

  • Processor: Intel Core i5-11400H (6 cores, up to 4.5 GHz)
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2050 4GB GDDR6
  • Display: 15.6" Full HD (1920×1080), 144 Hz IPS panel
  • Memory: 8GB DDR4-3200 (expandable up to 32GB)
  • Storage: 512GB PCIe NVMe SSD
  • OS: Windows 11 Home
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth, HDMI 2.0b, USB-C, USB-A, RJ45 LAN
  • Battery: 48 Wh
  • Weight: 2.3 kg

Performance

The Core i5-11400H and RTX 2050 combination is suitable for 1080p gaming at medium to high settings and general multitasking. The laptop handles games like Fortnite, Valorant, and Call of Duty well at these settings.

Display & Visuals

The 144 Hz panel makes gameplay smoother than standard 60 Hz displays. While color and brightness are standard for its tier, competitive gaming benefits greatly from the higher refresh rate.

Upgrade Potential

Dual SO-DIMM RAM slots and two M.2 storage slots allow for upgrades to 16-32GB RAM or additional SSD storage.

Pros & Cons

  • Pros: Affordable gaming laptop, 144 Hz display, RTX 2050 graphics, upgradeable RAM/storage.
  • Cons: Short battery life, some reported touchpad/battery issues.
