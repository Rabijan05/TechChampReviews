MSI Katana GF66 Review

Overview

The MSI Katana GF66 is a 15.6" gaming laptop offering respectable 1080p performance with an Intel Core i7 CPU and NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3060 GPU, giving a good balance of power and price.

Key Specifications

  • Processor: Intel Core i7‑12700H
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3060 6GB
  • Display: 15.6" Full HD (1920×1080), 144 Hz
  • Memory: 16GB DDR5
  • Storage: 1TB NVMe SSD
  • OS: Windows 11 Home
  • Connectivity: Wi‑Fi 6, Bluetooth, HDMI, USB‑C, RJ45 LAN
  • Battery: 53 Wh
  • Weight: ~2.3 kg

Performance

The combination of the i7‑12700H and RTX 3060 delivers solid 1080p gaming performance, capable of handling titles like Apex Legends and Borderlands 3 at high settings.

Display & Visuals

The 144 Hz panel helps with smooth gameplay visuals, though color accuracy is average for the class.

Upgrade Potential

RAM and storage are upgradeable, allowing future improvements to performance and capacity.

Pros & Cons

  • Pros: Strong gaming performance for price, upgradeable storage.
  • Cons: Build quality is modest, battery life isn’t great.
