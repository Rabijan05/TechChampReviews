Lenovo Legion 5 Review

Overview

The Lenovo Legion 5 is a versatile gaming laptop known for its solid thermals, strong performance, and excellent display options, making it a great mid‑range choice.

Key Specifications

  • Processor: Intel Core i7‑13650HX
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 5060 8GB
  • Display: 15" QHD+ OLED, 165 Hz
  • Memory: 16GB DDR5
  • Storage: 1TB SSD
  • OS: Windows 11 Home
  • Connectivity: Wi‑Fi 6E, Bluetooth, HDMI 2.1, USB‑C
  • Battery: ~80 Wh
  • Weight: ~2.4 kg

Performance

This configuration handles AAA gaming at 1080p and QHD with strong frame rates, especially utilizing DLSS and optimized drivers.

Display & Visuals

The 165 Hz QHD+ OLED display delivers vibrant colors and sharp detail, great for gaming and media consumption.

Upgrade Potential

Dual SO‑DIMM slots let you expand RAM and there’s extra SSD room for storage upgrades.

Pros & Cons

  • Pros: Great thermals, premium display, balanced performance.
  • Cons: Slightly heavier, battery life varies with OLED brightness.
