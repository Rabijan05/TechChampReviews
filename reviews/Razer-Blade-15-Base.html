Razer Blade 15 Base Review

Overview

The Razer Blade 15 Base is a sleek, premium 15.6" gaming laptop that combines portability with good performance. Featuring Intel Core i7 and NVIDIA RTX 3060, it’s ideal for gamers who want power in a compact design.

Key Specifications

  • Processor: Intel Core i7‑11800H
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3060 6GB
  • Display: 15.6" Full HD, 144 Hz
  • Memory: 16GB DDR4
  • Storage: 512GB NVMe SSD
  • OS: Windows 11 Home
  • Connectivity: Wi‑Fi 6, Bluetooth, HDMI 2.1, USB‑C
  • Battery: 80 Wh
  • Weight: ~2.1 kg

Performance

Handles 1080p gaming smoothly, including AAA titles, while keeping a sleek form factor. Good thermals despite compact chassis.

Display & Visuals

Bright 144 Hz panel offers smooth visuals for competitive gaming and daily use.

Upgrade Potential

RAM and storage are upgradeable, but options are more limited than bulkier gaming laptops.

Pros & Cons

  • Pros: Premium design, portable, good 1080p gaming performance.
  • Cons: Pricey for base configuration, limited upgradability.
