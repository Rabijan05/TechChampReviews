Lenovo Legion Slim 5 Review

Overview

The Lenovo Legion Slim 5 combines Ryzen 7 and RTX 4060 in a slim, portable design, offering solid gaming and productivity performance without the bulk of standard gaming laptops.

Key Specifications

  • Processor: AMD Ryzen 7 7840HS
  • Graphics: NVIDIA RTX 4060 8GB
  • Display: 15.6" Full HD, 165 Hz
  • Memory: 16GB DDR5
  • Storage: 1TB SSD
  • OS: Windows 11 Home
  • Connectivity: Wi‑Fi 6E, Bluetooth, HDMI, USB‑C
  • Battery: 80 Wh
  • Weight: 2.0 kg

Performance

Capable of running modern AAA titles at 1080p high settings and excellent for productivity tasks.

Display & Visuals

High-refresh 165 Hz panel ensures smooth gameplay, and colors are vibrant for media consumption.

Upgrade Potential

RAM and storage are user-upgradeable via accessible slots.

Pros & Cons

  • Cons: Slightly heavier than ultrabooks, battery moderate under gaming load.
