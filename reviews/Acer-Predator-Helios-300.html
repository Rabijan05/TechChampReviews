Acer Predator Helios 300 Review

Overview

The Acer Predator Helios 300 is a strong mid-range gaming laptop with Intel Core i7 and NVIDIA RTX 3060, delivering excellent 1080p performance at a very competitive price.

Key Specifications

  • Processor: Intel Core i7‑12700H
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3060 6GB
  • Display: 15.6" Full HD, 165 Hz
  • Memory: 16GB DDR5
  • Storage: 512GB NVMe SSD
  • OS: Windows 11 Home
  • Connectivity: Wi‑Fi 6, Bluetooth, HDMI, USB‑C/A
  • Battery: 59 Wh
  • Weight: ~2.5 kg

Performance

Excellent for high-frame-rate 1080p gaming and multitasking. Handles modern AAA titles with high settings smoothly.

165 Hz panel gives smooth visuals with minimal motion blur for competitive gaming.
